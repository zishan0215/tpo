About Jamia Millia Islamia

Jamia Millia Islamia was founded at Aligarh in the United Province, India in 1920 during the Khilafat and Non-Cooperation Movement in response to Gandhiji's call to boycott government supported educational institutions.
Jamia moved from Aligarh to Delhi in the year 1925 and was made a central university by Parlimentary act of 1988. Today, Jamia Millia Islamia is one of the most prominent and promising Central Universitities of the country.
Jamia has been recently awareded 'A' Grade by NAAC.(National Assessment and Accreditation Council)

About Department of Computer Engineering

Started in 2000, the Department of Computeer Engineering stands one of the leading departments at Jamia.
The department successfully runs two undergarduate courses -B. Tech and B.E. Providing students with balanced theoritical and practical expertise. The Ph.D program boost of research scholars working in the field of Networking, Data Mining, AI etc.
The department actively supports various technical and non-technical causues, promoting student initated activies
